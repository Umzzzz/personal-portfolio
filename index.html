An insight about me...

A third year undergraduate student, studying BSc Web Development at Manchester Metropolitan University. Currently I aspire to gain valuable experience through a work placement within the area of study I have gained knowledge in. My interests lie in it but also compliment well with other sectors such as Business or Finance.

My reasons for studying this degree programme is not only due to personal interest and goals, but also to become familiar with updates in the technological field as the world we live in today revolves around media and technology. Industries are looking for fresh talent with new and various ideas to bring to their companies. Becoming successful in the work I do is a must and thus, I am working hard with motivation and to the best of my abilities to aim high in all of my units so that I get excellent results. Overall I am aiming to get a 1:1.

In regards to future developments, through job progression I would want a long term career in Website Development. I would like to gain the best experience in a reputable and prestige company within the UK to build on a portfolio. However, through enhancing job prospects, I aspire to own a firm with my brother abroad to web design for independent and well-known companies. Future aspirations is one of the reasons why I am looking for a graduate placement as I believe this is the best way to gain work experience and develop my skills further. To work practically in the work force to gain experience is just as important as having a piece of paper that states 'graduate', and the work fields I am intrigued to work in are Website Development, Project Management , UX and SEO.
